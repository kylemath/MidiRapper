How to use:

  1. Type or paste your poem in the text area above
  2. Click "Connect MIDI Keyboard" and select your device
  3. Play notes on your MIDI keyboard - each note plays the note and speaks the next word from your poem at that note's pitch, advancing sequentially through the poem (perfect for rapping!)
  4. Play C, C#, D, E minor, etc. and the words will advance: word 1, word 2, word 3, word 4...
